Welcome! to JB'22

A bequest being nurtured by the accordant individuals of JUET aims to team up the brains of programmers, designers, application developers and rookies in the realm of programming for the substantial development of a hack. You’ll have all the opportunity to conceptualize your ideas with the learned individuals of industry, create a product, innovate new things, and most importantly, enjoy memorable moments with your friends. One thing which goes without saying for a hackathon like JUET Builds is that you stand a very good chance of networking with working professionals and community leaders.
